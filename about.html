About Potato Host 🥔

Potato Host was started with a simple vision: Web hosting should be free, accessible, and independent.


Instead of relying on multi-billion dollar cloud monopolies, we power our hosting platform using repurposed hardware—literally a "potato PC" running from home! By combining home servers with modern cloud layers like Cloudflare, we deliver stable static web hosting at zero cost to you.


Our Mission

To help students, new developers, and hobbyists launch their first web applications without ever worrying about surprise hosting bills or credit card inputs.
