Yes! We do not ask for credit cards. Hosting static sites (HTML, CSS, JS) costs us almost nothing on our hardware setups.

What are the limits per website?

Every registered user receives 100MB of pure NVMe/SSD storage and unmetered bandwith optimized via Cloudflare caching layer.
